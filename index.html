Process

How Numen works

Multi-path exploration, symbolic verification, zero tolerance for unverified outputs.

01

Input your problem

Type the equation directly or upload a photo of your handwritten problem. Numen's OCR extracts the math — and you can edit any symbol it missed.

02

MCTS exploration

Monte Carlo Tree Search explores multiple solution paths simultaneously, selecting the most promising approach for your specific problem type.

03

Symbolic verification

Every candidate solution is verified mathematically using SymPy before being accepted. If it can't be proven, it's rejected — no guessing.

04

Solution + explanation

You get the verified answer, the full step-by-step reasoning chain, confidence score, and a plain-language explanation.
